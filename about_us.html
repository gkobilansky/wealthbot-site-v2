About us

  • Home
  • About us

We are two brothers asking the question - "Is Fintech Ready for open source?". Wealthbot.io is our way of answering this question. Between us we have over 30 years experience in finance and technology. We've been working together since 2012 on projects ranging from iPhone apps to wearable technology.

Why?

We believe the future of wealth management is the cyborg advisor. A human that uses technology to enhance relationships and scale personalized service.

Every day I speak with independent Investment Advisors looking for a portfolio management solution that will help them compete with robo-advisors.

Today anyone can use Wordpress to launch a website. With wealthbot.io, we make it easy for qualified individuals to launch their own virtual investment advising firm.

The future of wealth management is the cyborg advisor. The future of software is open source.
